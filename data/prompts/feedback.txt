Analyze the candidate's answer provided below based on the user's interview profile ({role}, {seniority}, {domain}).

Candidate's Answer:
"{answer}"

Evaluation Criteria:
- **Clarity:** How clear and concise was the answer?
- **Technical Depth:** How well did the candidate demonstrate their technical knowledge?
- **Relevance:** Was the answer relevant to the question?
- **Confidence:** How confident did the candidate appear?

Scoring:
Score each of the above dimensions on a 1-5 scale (1=Poor, 5=Excellent).

Feedback:
- Provide exactly two strengths observed in the answer.
- Provide exactly two areas for improvement.

Output Format:
Return a JSON object with the following keys: "scores", "strengths", "improvements".
The "scores" value should be a dictionary of the evaluation criteria and their scores.
